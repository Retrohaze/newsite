About Us

We make

your fitness goals

SUNDOFIT was started in the year 2011 to make exercise, fun and a way of life! We are the only stand alone fitness consultancy which offers a variety of fitness and wellness programs, that helps educate the community, motivate people to make a progressive change and help them integrate these changes into their lifestyle. We strive to teach clients something new about fitness. Every session at SUNDOFIT will be a step towards making better choices about their fitness and overall health for life.

Customised Training

Here, when asked for personalised training to accompolish the goal, we take extra care to do the same. Because here at Sundofit, your goal is our goal.

We care about your comfort, if you need it, we do it!

The reason behind building this company was to make India fit and here we are pushing the boundary everyday. Join us !

There is really no age to be fit.

Being fit is what makes us human. We understand the busy schedule you all are in, but to help you be fit, Sundofit will provide you witht the timetable that fits your free time. Now buys isn't a reason to getaway from being fit!

Our classes

  • 01

    KK - Nagar - Sivan Park.

    Physical fitness is a state of health and well-being and, more specifically, having the ability to perform effortlessly in sports, occupations and day to day activities. We at Sundofit KK nagar, strive to make our fitness partners to discover the same. Come visit our location at KK Nagar, situated very beautifully at Shivan park and join us in our journey towards effortless living.

  • 02

    Virugambakkam - Cerus Appswamy & YS Enclave

    The Virugabakkam location is situated in the heart of Cerus Appaswamy & YS Enclave, where the fitness sessions happening have been solely developed with the satisfaction of our fitness partners in mind. It is a galore of self-motivated people working together to get fit through an easy and enjoyable process.

  • 03

    Iyyappanthangal - Corp. Park

    Situated with close proximity to the peace and quiet of the Chennai corporation park, the Iyyanppanthangal location is the best place for you to come and join our fitness partners as they exercise towards a fit body and mind.

  • 04

    Kattupakkam - Prestige Bella Vista

    Come join us at this session located in Kattupakkam’s own Prestige Bella Vista with its beautiful architectural intricacies and modern setup, aiding us in achieving the motivation and concentration required for a good day’s work out.

  • 05

    Marina Beach - Nr. Gandhi Statue

    As Chennai city boasts about having the greatest shoreline in Asia, we at Sundofit boast of having the greatest sessions at the most beautiful marina beach amidst the cool breezes and relaxing sound of waves.

  • 06

    Porur - Osian Chlorophyl

    Osian Chlorophyll in Porur offers the best setting for the most energy filled exercise sessions, with improved stamina from breathing the fresh and clean air, away from the city. Come join us there and let’s get fit together.

  • 07

    Anna Nagar - Tower Park

    Anna Nagar tower park is a place filled with old charm and serenity. The pathways reverberate with the soulful melodies of old songs which makes exercising there, the most delightful activity. The trees there make the place cool even during the warmest day. Come on and join us at this joy-filled place to work towards a healthier life.
